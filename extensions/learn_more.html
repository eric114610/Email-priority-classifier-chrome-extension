About Email Priority Instant Classifier (EPIC)

What is Email Priority Instant Classifier (EPIC)?

Email Priority Instant Classifier (later refered as EPIC) is a Chrome extension designed to automatic help you classify emails' priority efficiently using AI-powered solutions. It integrates seamlessly with Gmail, offering smart, context-aware classification to help you decide which emails to prioritize and which can wait.

With EPIC, it classifies emails based on its importancy, urgency, and even customize the AI's classification strategy to fit your personal behavior.

About Section

This section in the popup gives you a quick overview of EPIC and its main features. You can click the "Details" button to open this page for more information.

Record Usage Section

Here you can see how many reply records (emails) you have stored out of the 100 available slots. The progress bar visually indicates your usage. Use the "Manage" button to view or delete your records.

Categories Section

Your records are organized into up to five categories for visualization. This section help you quickly monitor emails' status.

Customize Prompt Section

Tailor the AI's classifier by setting a custom prompt. You can enter up to 100 words to guide the backend AI, ensuring the classification results match your goal and requirements. Click "Apply" to save your custom prompt. Please note that you can apply your custom prompt with or without re-running all existing records' classification results.
